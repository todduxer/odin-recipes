Swedish Meatballs

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground beef, bread crumbs, milk, and butter. Mix well.
  2. Shape the mixture into small balls.
  3. In a large pot, heat the flour and water over medium heat. Stir until the flour is dissolved.
  4. Add the beef broth and tomato sauce. Stir until the sauce is thickened.
  5. Add the meatballs to the sauce. Stir until the meatballs are heated through.
