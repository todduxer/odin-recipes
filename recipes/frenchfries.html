French Fries

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of potatoes
  • 1 cup of oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes into thin slices.
  2. Put the potatoes in a bowl of water to remove the excess starch.
  3. Put the potatoes in a pot of water and boil for 10 minutes.
  4. Put the potatoes in a bowl of ice water to stop the cooking process.
  5. Put the potatoes in a pot of oil and fry for 10 minutes.
  6. Put the potatoes in a bowl of salt and pepper to taste.
