French Fries

Description

French fries are a popular snack food made from sliced potatoes that are deep-fried in oil. They are often served with a side of ketchup or other dipping sauces.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Slice the potatoes
  2. Deep-fry the potatoes
  3. Serve the potatoes
